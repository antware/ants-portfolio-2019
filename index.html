AboutMe

Hi there, I'm Ant.

In fact it's Antony John Ware, but just plain ol' Ant will do.

I am a digital graphic and web designer, shortly relocating to Cork Ireland, from Durban, South Africa. I'm very passionate about Frontend Web Development and all things web. I am dedicated to continuously keeping up with industry trends and keeping myself skilled in the latest technologies. I have spent the last 24 years looking into various incarnations of monitors, starting with administering databases, then creating maps in Microstation and ArcView, as well as performing data queries in ArcGIS, before moving onto desktop publishing and graphic design using the Adobe Suite of products and then finally designing websites. My first website was done in Microsoft Frontpage (yeah cringe), eventually moving over to Dreamweaver (starting with the WYSIWYG editor and finally transitioning to the Code Editor), and then onto WordPress and now finally handcoding HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Web apps based in JavaScript is the next phase on my journey. It's been quite a ride for sure.

I enjoy every step of the design and development process, from discussion and collaboration to concept and execution, but I find the most satisfaction in seeing the finished product do everything it was created to do. Through my years of experience as a web/graphic designer I have acquired the skills, knowledge and experience necessary to be a valued member of your team.
